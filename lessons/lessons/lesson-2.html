Four-Point Tie-Down Overview

  1. Position the chair facing forward.
  2. Attach front restraints to solid frame points.
  3. Attach rear restraints, remove slack, then tighten evenly.
  4. Apply lap/shoulder belt to the passenger (never to the chair).
